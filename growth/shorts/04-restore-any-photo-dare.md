# Short Video 04 — "Send me your worst old photo"

**Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
**Length:** 35 seconds
**Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical
**Shooting time:** ~15 min. Editing: ~40 min.

## Concept

Engagement-bait in the honest sense — invites viewers to post their own
damaged photos in the comments. Comments = algorithm boost. Every comment
gets a reply with a free preview, which = viewer becomes invested, which
= viewer buys or shares. This is the format that TikTok/Reels algorithms
love most.

## Edit notes

- This video lives or dies on comments. The voiceover at 24–30s is a
  direct call to action — make it LOUD in the mix.
- The "send me your worst" line (0–5s) is the hook. It needs to punch.
  Re-record that line 3–4 times with different emphases and pick the
  sharpest.
- At 19–24s the screen recording of artgen.site should show a REAL
  upload with a visible damaged photo and visible watermarked preview
  appearing. Do not fake this with a mockup — viewers spot fakes.

## Comment response plan (important — video only works if you actually reply)

**When someone drops a photo in the comments:**

1. Reply with "On it 🫡" within 30 minutes
2. Within 1 hour: download their photo, run through artgen.site, get
   watermarked preview
3. Reply-comment with the watermarked preview as an image:
   > Here's the preview — full HD without the watermark is $6.90 at
   > artgen.site/ai-hd (or $29 if you want a human retoucher). Or just
   > save this preview for free, no card needed.

**Keep a tally.** Screenshot the best comments + your responses for
future videos ("I restored 50 people's photos last week — here are the
top 5").

## Post-production checklist

- [ ] The on-screen prompt "Comment the worst damaged photo" stays up
      for at least 6 seconds — long enough for a scroller to register it
- [ ] Pin the top reply after posting: "Drop your photo below 👇"
- [ ] Set up a 15-min reminder to check comments for the first 2 hours
      after posting — that's the engagement-velocity window
