# Short Video 01 — "6 photos, 60 seconds, one free preview tool"

**Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts (same MP4 to all)
**Length:** 55–60 seconds
**Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical (1080×1920)
**Shooting time:** ~20 minutes. Editing: ~45 minutes.

## Concept (one sentence)

Rapid-fire before→after reveals of 6 historical portraits, cadenced to an
increasingly emotional beat, ending with the tool URL on screen.

## What you'll shoot

All 6 reveals are **screen recordings of artgen.site/ai-hd processing
real photos**. No human on camera. Phone screen mirror or QuickTime to
record Mac desktop browser, then rotate to vertical in editing.

### Photos to use (in this order — it matters)

1. **Fleetwood** — dramatic contrast recovery (opener must punch)
2. **Woolf 1902** — extreme fade, reveal feels like ghost emerging
3. **Carver 1910** — clean studio portrait (breather beat)
4. **Sofia Wallin scratched** — visible scratch removal (movement catches eye)
5. **Nielsen 1908** — group portrait with clear faces (emotional peak)
6. **Li Fu Lee** — non-Western face (diversity + memorable close)

Before URLs: `artgen.site/examples/old-photos/pair-<name>-before.jpg`
After URLs: `artgen.site/examples/new-photos/pair-<name>-after.jpg`

> they could look like.
>
> Upload any photo you have. Watermarked preview shows up in under a minute.

Record with an iPhone in a quiet room with a t-shirt over the microphone
area (cheap de-essing). Conversational pace, no hype.

## Music

**TikTok / Reels:** Pick a trending melancholy-but-warm instrumental.
Search the platform's audio library for "piano cinematic emotional" or
"family memories" — use one with 10k+ current videos so the algorithm
recognizes it as a trend. DO NOT use copyrighted music for YouTube Shorts.

**YouTube:** Use YouTube Audio Library free tracks. Search "emotional
piano" or "warm cinematic." Safe tracks: _"Where the Memories Sleep"_,
_"Vintage Memories"_, _"Heartwarming"_.

## Edit notes

- Every crossfade between BEFORE→AFTER should be exactly 0.5s. Faster
  feels AI-genny, slower feels like a stock video.
- Add a subtle "old film" grain overlay at 8% opacity for cohesion
  across clips of different eras.
- Caption text should be in `Inter` or `Helvetica Bold`, white with a
  2px black stroke for legibility on any photo background.
- Export as MP4 H.264, 1080×1920, 30fps, bitrate 10 Mbps.
