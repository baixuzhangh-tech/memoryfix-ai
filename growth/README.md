Everything in this folder is **ready to use**. You don't need to write a single
word — just follow the handoff checklist and paste/shoot what's here.

## Core strategy (one paragraph)

Your unfair advantage is the **free watermarked preview**. Every Reddit post
and every short-video ends with the same single-sentence CTA:

> "Free watermarked preview at artgen.site — only pay if you love it."

We DO NOT fabricate personal family stories ("my grandfather" etc.) around
photos that aren't yours. That's the fastest way to get banned on Reddit and
tank brand trust. Instead we use three frames:

1. **Founder voice** — "I built this, here's what it does, free preview"
2. **Community service** — "Drop your damaged photo in the comments, I'll run
   a free preview and post the result"
3. **Educational / teardown** — "Here's how modern AI restoration works, 5
   things to watch out for"

All three convert. Fake grandma stories do not.

## Minimum viable week (if you only do 20% of this)

If you only do 5 things total, do these in this order:

1. Launch a clean Reddit account and run `reddit/01-photorestoration-launch.md`
2. Shoot + post `shorts/01-6-photos-60-seconds.md` on TikTok + Reels + YouTube Shorts (same file)
3. Launch `reddit/02-sideproject-show.md` on `r/SideProject`
4. Run `reddit/03-community-invite-freepreview.md` on `r/photorestoration`
5. Shoot + post `shorts/02-how-ai-restoration-works.md`

That alone should drive 300–1500 preview-starts in week 1 if even one of them
catches. Everything else in this folder is compounding on top of those five.
